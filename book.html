How to book a flight ticket and hotel for foreigner to visit Cambodia?

We all know a way how to book a flight ticket.But,this is an article that will tell people who don't have any idea about how to book a flight ticket.I'll talk about the flight to go to Cambodia.

First of all,the passenger need to know about the date that he or she would like to visit Cambodia.Second,you need to choose a booking platform that make sure that it is verify by the government or guarantee by the company to make sure that they don't scam you.In this case,you have to choose a destination to Cambodia in your booking platform,and selected the date and time that you want.While searching you can choose which airport that you like to.Cambodia have two airport,one located in Phnom Penh called Pochentong Airport,another one called Siem Reao Airport which is located in Siem Reap.Both of the airports are located in Cambodia,but it is base on the purpose of the passenger.

Next step,the passenger need to make sure that you have a passport in order to book a flight ticket.Then you put the informationt that they required, review and confirm that all your informationt hat youhave put in are correct,make a payment,and you will receive a confirmation letter with your e-ticket.

To add on,the booking platform are provide by many comapny such as Trip.com,Acoda,Cambodia Airway,Air Asia or the platform that suit you.

Moreover,if you want to book a flight with hotel it would be cheaper than book only flight in case that you have a permanent date that you would like to go.Although some hotel don't allow you to cancel,but make sure to book a hotel that you can cancel with fine and get the money that you pay them back.

In case for people who like to live in Cambodia rather than come to have holiday,Make sure to research how to ask for visa to live in Cambodia without scared and spend the day in Cambodia peacefully.
